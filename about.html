What have I learnt?

Well, what have I learnt completing this course? I know I can identify HTML, CSS and Javascript files. I can locate a vast range of resources and perhaps have some inkling how to achieve an aim. I know what people are talking about even if I'm incredibly slow myself.

I am a qualified school teacher; I have planned and taught thousands of lessons. The sheer volume of information and pace at which this course runs is phenomenal. Hats off to everyone who kept up and managed the pace. I have cried and am still overwhelmed by all of it. It is learning another language, with all its technical terms, and I have found it incredibly hard to keep up.

I know I can copy a template and populate it with my own content. I know I need to use better quality images than snips and I should have had hovering text over my useful information section. I still have a lot to learn.

Will I be going forward to find a career as a Web developer? I am not 100% sure at present. I like tech but I think I may be more suited to a different area.
